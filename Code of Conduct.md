# Pulsar Engine Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in the Pulsar Engine community.

This Code of Conduct exists to support healthy collaboration and is enforced alongside the Community Policy.

---

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and professional
* Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people
* Assume good faith
* Communicate clearly and constructively

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
* Discrimination or exclusionary behavior
* Intimidation or sustained hostility
* Deliberate disruption of discussions

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action to address behavior that violates this Code of Conduct or the Community Policy, including moderation or restriction of participation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Pulsar Engine community spaces, including repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and chat platforms.
