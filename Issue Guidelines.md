# Issue Guidelines

## Purpose

Issues are for actionable work items and discussion related to Pulsar Engine development.

---

## Good Issues Include

* Clear problem descriptions
* Steps to reproduce bugs
* Performance data where relevant
* Minimal repro cases

---

## Not Appropriate for Issues

* General support requests (See support channels in the discord listed in the engine's README)
* Ideological debates (This does not belong in our community anywhere)
* Tooling arguments (This does not belong in our community anywhere)

---

## Maintainer Discretion

Maintainers may close, label, or redirect issues to keep the tracker useful and focused.
