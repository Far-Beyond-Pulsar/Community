# Contributing to Pulsar Engine

## Welcome

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Pulsar Engine. Contributions of all kinds are welcome, whether code, documentation, testing, or discussion.

---

## Getting Started

* Browse open issues
* Ask questions in discussions
* Start small; improvements are iterative

---

## Pull Requests

* Keep changes focused
* Explain intent and tradeoffs
* Expect review and iteration

Maintainers may request changes or decline PRs to protect the long-term health of the project.

---

## Expectations

* There is no obligation to contribute in order to use Pulsar
* Contributions are reviewed on technical merit
* Experimental ideas are welcome but may not be merged
